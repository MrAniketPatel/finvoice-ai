# FinVoice.AI Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Mission**: To make financial management as natural as conversation, giving everyone the power to understand their money and make confident decisions.

**Vision**: Become the most trusted and intuitive finance management platform, powered by AI and voice technology.

**Values**:
- Clarity over complexity
- Trust through transparency
- Innovation with purpose
- Accessibility for all

## Design System

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors**:
- Navy Blue: `#1B263B` - Trust, stability, professionalism
- Soft Green: `#A8DADC` - Growth, prosperity, calm
- Teal: `#71C7B8` - Innovation, balance, clarity

**Neutral Colors**:
- Off White: `#F7F9FA` - Clean, spacious, modern
- Dark Gray: `#2E2E2E` - Readable, professional
- White: `#FFFFFF` - Pure, simple

**Accent Colors**:
- Success Green: `#71C7B8`
- Warning Orange: `#FFA500`
- Error Red: `#FF6B6B`

### Typography

**Font Family**: Inter, Segoe UI, system fonts
- Clean, modern, highly readable
- Excellent for both display and body text

**Font Weights**:
- Regular (400): Body text
- Medium (500): Subheadings
- Semibold (600): Section titles
- Bold (700): Main headings
- Extra Bold (800): Hero titles

**Font Sizes**:
- Hero: 56px (mobile: 38px)
- H1: 42px (mobile: 32px)
- H2: 32px (mobile: 24px)
- H3: 22px
- Body: 16px
- Small: 14px

### Spacing

**Generous spacing for readability**:
- Section padding: 100px (mobile: 60px)
- Card padding: 40px (mobile: 25px)
- Element gaps: 20-30px
- Micro spacing: 8-16px

### Border Radius

**Soft, rounded corners**:
- Large cards: 24px
- Medium cards: 20px
- Buttons: 12-14px
- Small elements: 10px
- Pills: 50px

### Shadows

**Soft, layered shadows**:
- Light: `0 4px 20px rgba(27, 38, 59, 0.08)`
- Medium: `0 8px 30px rgba(27, 38, 59, 0.15)`
- Heavy: `0 12px 40px rgba(27, 38, 59, 0.25)`

### Animations

**Smooth, purposeful transitions**:
- Duration: 0.3s - 0.5s
- Easing: ease, ease-in-out
- Hover effects: translateY(-2px to -5px)
- Fade in: 0.6s
- Slide in: 0.8s

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

**Friendly but Professional**:
- "Welcome back!" not "Login successful"
- "Let's track your expenses" not "Add transaction"
- "You're all caught up!" not "No pending items"

**Clear and Concise**:
- Short sentences
- Active voice
- No jargon unless necessary
- Explain technical terms

**Supportive and Encouraging**:
- "Great job staying on top of payments!"
- "Your balance is looking healthy"
- "Let's add your first transaction"

**Trustworthy**:
- Transparent about data usage
- Clear about security measures
- Honest about limitations

### Writing Guidelines

**Do's**:
✅ Use conversational language
✅ Focus on benefits, not features
✅ Use positive framing
✅ Be specific with numbers
✅ Include helpful context

**Don'ts**:
❌ Use technical jargon
❌ Be overly formal
❌ Use negative language
❌ Make empty promises
❌ Overwhelm with information

### Example Copy

**Hero Section**:
- "Manage Your Finances With Intelligence"
- "Track income, expenses, and never miss a payment"

**Features**:
- "Smart Dashboard" not "Data Visualization"
- "Payment Alerts" not "Notification System"
- "Balance Sheets" not "Financial Reports"

**Actions**:
- "Get Started Free" not "Sign Up"
- "Add Transaction" not "Create Entry"
- "Mark as Paid" not "Update Status"

**Feedback**:
- "Transaction added successfully!" not "Record created"
- "Profile updated!" not "Changes saved"
- "Payment reminder set" not "Alert configured"

## Do's and Don'ts

### Visual Design

**Do**:
✅ Use soft shadows
✅ Round corners generously
✅ Add smooth transitions
✅ Create visual hierarchy
✅ Use whitespace liberally

**Don't**:
❌ Use harsh shadows
❌ Sharp corners everywhere
❌ Jarring animations
❌ Cluttered layouts
❌ Cramped spacing

### Content

**Do**:
✅ Tell stories
✅ Show empathy
✅ Provide value
✅ Be conversational
✅ Guide users

**Don't**:
❌ List features only
❌ Be robotic
❌ Waste time
❌ Use corporate speak
❌ Confuse users
